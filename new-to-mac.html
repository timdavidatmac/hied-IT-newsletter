New to Mac?

A curated set of Apple resources to help you get started — and get the most from your Mac.

Whether you're switching from Windows, setting up a new device, or looking for helpful resources to share with your team or students — this page brings together Apple's best starting points in one place.

Getting Started

  • Apple Guide Mac User Guide Everything you need to know about using your Mac — features, settings, and step-by-step instructions for everyday tasks.
  • Apple Guide Switch from Windows to Mac A practical guide for anyone coming from a Windows environment — covering setup, your favorite apps, and what's different (and better) on Mac.
  • Apple Guide What's It Called on My Mac? Not sure what to call something you see on screen? This guide names everything — from the menu bar to the Dock to Spotlight.
  • Apple Guide Mac Keyboard Shortcuts A comprehensive reference for keyboard shortcuts across macOS — a quick bookmark that pays off every day.

For Education

  • Apple Education Mac Starter Guide for Education A beginner-friendly guide designed for educators and students — covering the essentials of getting set up and productive on Mac.
  • Apple Education Get to Know Your Mac — Free Live Training Free instructor-led virtual sessions for PC-to-Mac switchers and anyone new to Mac. A great resource to share with faculty, staff, and students.

Accessibility

  • Accessibility Apple Accessibility Features Apple builds accessibility into every product — including features for vision, hearing, mobility, speech, and cognitive needs. Explore what's available across Mac, iPhone, iPad, and more.

Video Resources

  • YouTube Apple Support — YouTube Channel Short, clear how-to videos from Apple covering setup, features, tips, and troubleshooting across all Apple devices. Perfect for sharing with end users.
  • YouTube Playlist Apple Support — Mac Get the most from your Mac. Learn how to keep macOS up to date, explore the latest features, and find out how to customize them to work best for you.
  • YouTube Playlist College Essentials — Apple Support Scan text into Notes. Multitask with Split View. Set a custom Focus. Discover tips for staying productive in college with Mac, iPad, and iPhone.

Get Help

  • Apple Support Get Support from Apple Contact Apple Support directly — find answers, schedule a repair, or connect with an Apple Specialist for help with any Apple product.
