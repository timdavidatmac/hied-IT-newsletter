⚠️ Action Suggested

Four Items to Address Before Fall

Office 2019 for Mac — Read-Only on July 13

Microsoft is ending support for Office 2019 on July 13, 2026. After that date, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook will open files in read-only mode. Read more →

Okta Verify Does Not Launch on macOS 27 Beta 1

Institutions using Okta for SSO or MFA should be aware that Okta Verify fails to launch on macOS 27 Beta 1. Watch for an Okta fix release before moving to Beta 2 testing.

Sign in with Apple and Hide My Email — New Domain This Summer

Later this summer, Sign in with Apple and iCloud+ Hide My Email will transition to a unified domain: private.icloud.com. IT teams managing firewall allow-lists or proxy rules for these services should plan for this change. Read more →

A12/A13 — Unpatchable SecureROM Vulnerability

Security researchers have published a BootROM exploit affecting Apple A12 and A13 chips — found in iPhone XR, XS, XS Max, 11, 11 Pro, 11 Pro Max, and early iPad models. This vulnerability cannot be patched via software update. Institutions with these devices in managed fleets should review their security posture accordingly. Read more →

What's New

🔥 Apple Training Promotion — Last Chance, Through June 30

Buy a $149 USD certification exam voucher and get a free practice exam voucher — both valid through October 31, 2026. The promotion ends June 30. Buy now →

🪛 MacBook Neo — Designed for Repair

A closer look at how MacBook Neo was built for long-term ownership. Key components — battery, speakers, trackpad, ports, and logic board — are individually serviceable with no two components stacked on top of one another. Apple supports organizational repair through its Self-Servicing Account Program, providing access to genuine parts, repair manuals, diagnostics, and tools. Read more →

Jamf Pro 11.29

Two notable updates in the latest Jamf Pro release. First, DDM-based software update management is now available for on-premise Jamf Pro deployments — not just Jamf Cloud. Second, the Simplified Setup for Platform SSO has been enhanced to support more flexible identity provider configurations. Release notes →

OS 27 Beta 2 Available

Beta 2 of iOS 27, iPadOS 27, macOS 27, watchOS 27, and visionOS 27 is now available via AppleSeed for IT. If you haven't enrolled your test devices yet, this is a good time to start — Apple needs IT administrator feedback early in the beta cycle. AppleSeed for IT →

🛫 End of an Era — AirPort Utility

AirPort Utility will be removed from the App Store and macOS with the release of iOS 27 and macOS 27 this fall. If your institution still has AirPort base stations in service, now is the time to plan a migration to a supported networking solution.

Jamf DDM Explorer

A free app from Jamf that helps IT administrators learn and understand Apple's Declarative Device Management framework. Browse published declaration types for current and beta operating systems, build and deploy test payloads directly from the app, and view live declarative status reports from enrolled test devices. Especially useful now that OS 27 DDM updates are available in beta. App Store →  ·  Learn more →

SimpleSSL — Certificate Management for Mac

A free Mac app for viewing and managing SSL certificates, with an optional Pro upgrade ($4.99). Useful for IT admins auditing TLS certificates across managed environments. App Store →
